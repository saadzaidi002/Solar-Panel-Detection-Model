Pipeline

How It Works

Our AI pipeline transforms raw satellite imagery into precise solar panel detections using a state-of-the-art Oriented Bounding Box (OBB) model.

1

Satellite Image Input

High-resolution satellite or aerial imagery is fed into the detection pipeline as input.

2

YOLOv8-OBB Inference

The trained YOLOv8 model with Oriented Bounding Box detection processes the image in real-time.

3

Panel Localization

Detected solar panels are precisely localized with rotated bounding boxes that match panel orientation.

4

Analysis & Insights

Detection results are analyzed to provide panel counts, confidence scores, and change-over-time metrics.
